About Infinity Search

What We Do

Infinity Search Solo is an open source metasearch engine with a special focus on privacy and efficiency. All it really does is proxies results from Bing and Wikipedia to you with a customizable user interface.

How Infinity Search Solo Works

When you search on our site, we take results from Bing and Wikipedia, organize them, and display them directly to you without logging any identifiable information about you.

How We Make Money

Infinity Search Solo doesn't make any money but we are the creators of Infinity Search, a subscription based search engine.

About Our Company

Infinity Search Solo is part of Innovare Technologies and we are just a small team working to improve how people search online.
